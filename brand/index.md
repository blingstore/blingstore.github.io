## Typography

### Fonts
- **Primary Font**: Metropolis (for headings, titles, and important text)
- **Secondary Font**: Helvetica (for body text and other elements)

### Sizes
- **Headings (h1 to h6):**
  - h1: 2.5rem
  - h2: 2rem
  - h3: 1.75rem
  - h4: 1.5rem
  - h5: 1.25rem
  - h6: 1rem
- **Body text**: 1rem (16px)
